The Rule Book

Read and follow these rules to maintain a welcoming and safe community. Violations will lead to warnings or permanent bans. We allow using and talking about client modifications like BetterDiscord and Vencord.

📜

Discord's ToS & Community Guidelines

  • You must be at least 13 years old
  • No self-bots, nitro snipers, or API exploits
  • No joking about raids, doxxes, or swatting
  • No scams, malware, or malicious links
  • No leaking private information (IPs, addresses, phone numbers, IDs)
  • Voice recordings require consent from all participants
🔞

No NSFW / Gore / Explicit Content

  • Zero tolerance for NSFW jokes, memes, or pictures
  • No NSFL (Not Safe For Life) or gore content
  • No e-dating, flirting, or hitting on members
  • No suggestive or cropped NSFW content
  • No sexualizing minors - zero tolerance, zero appeals
  • No bait content or optical illusions
🤝

Be Respectful To All Members

  • No trolling, toxicity, or arguments in public channels
  • No death threats, racism, sexism, or hate speech
  • No xenophobic content (swastikas, etc.)
  • Avoid sensitive topics: politics, religion, war
  • No ragebait or intentional drama
  • Discuss controversial topics in DMs only
🚫

Don't Spam / Be A Nuisance

  • No spamming messages, emotes, or reactions
  • No excessive pinging or VC soundboard spam
  • No loud/bright media or ear-rape content
  • No mini-modding or backseat moderating
  • No bypassing automod filters
  • Brain rot words (sigma, skibidi, gyatt) are blocked
👤

Discord Profile Rules

  • Profiles must follow all server rules
  • No NSFW, hate speech, or racist content in profile
  • Name, bio, PFP, and pronouns are all moderated
  • No impersonating staff or XploPlayZYTube
  • Impersonation of YouTubers may be moderated
📢

Advertising Rules

  • No DM advertising or unsolicited invites
  • Advertise only in designated promotional channels
  • No server invites, social media links, or referral links in chat
  • Violating this rule will result in punishment
⚖️

Don't Find Loopholes

  • Intentionally bending rules will result in moderation
  • Not all rules are perfectly defined - avoid exploiting that
  • Report unfair moderator treatment to managers
  • DM XploPlayZYTube for Admin+ complaints

Punishment System

To maintain a fair and safe environment, we follow a structured punishment system. Repeated infractions lead to increased penalties.

⚠️

Warning

First infraction results in a formal warning. Second infraction escalates to a kick.

👞

Kick

Serious or repeat offenses result in a kick. Re-offending after a kick leads to a ban.

🔨

Ban

First ban lasts 2 days. Second infraction results in a permanent (infinite) ban.

🔇

Mute

Communication infractions result in a 2-day mute. Second infraction lasts 14 days.

Ban Appeal

Got Banned?

If you believe you were banned unfairly or would like to appeal your ban, please fill out the form below. We will review your appeal and get back to you within 7 days.

Appeal Guidelines

  • Be honest and respectful in your appeal
  • Explain what led to your ban
  • Show that you understand why you were banned
  • Demonstrate that you will follow the rules if unbanned
  • Do not argue or make excuses
  • Appeals from alternate accounts may be rejected
Note: Repeated or disrespectful appeals may result in permanent bans from appeal submissions. Some bans are permanent and cannot be appealed.
